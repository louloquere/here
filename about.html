About the website

Studies have shown that simply talking about our problems and sharing our negative emotions with someone we trust can be profoundly healing—reducing stress, strengthening our immune system, and reducing physical and emotional distress (Pennebaker, Kiecolt-Glaser, & Glaser, 1988).

There’s a reason people love going to the zoo and aquarium. We want to get closer to animals, even if it means giving up some of our own comfort.

Mental help is the place where you can do just that,we will be compassionate with users and let them vent their frustrations. If they understand the process, they will enter into a positive state of mind and feel more empowered to overcome whatever challenges, problems or frustrations come along on their journey.
The goal is to empathize with the user and guide them through an emotional experience without becoming intrusive.


The Developer

The goal is to create a safe place for people who are looking for advice on a topic, but don't have anybody to go to that can help them. It's this stage where I'm a temporary friend to the user and I come in, give advice and send them on their way with the appropriate resources for their age or situation.
